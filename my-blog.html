sondorbon tour in summer.

author: nayok asraf ali kha

St. Martin's island, the only coral island in Bangladesh, located in the northeastern part of the Bay of Bengal,forming the southernmost part of Bangladesh. St. Martin's Island is located about 9 km south of Teknaf in Cox'sBazar district and 8 km west of the coast of Myanmar at the mouth of the Naf River. It is also locally known aNarikel Jinjira because of the abundance of coconuts. Many years ago, in unfavorable weather conditions, an Arabian merchant ship carrying Daru Chini(Cinnamon) crashed into a huge rock under the water, causing the cinnamon to spread throughout the island, later renamed St. Martin's Island as 'Cinnamon' Island.
